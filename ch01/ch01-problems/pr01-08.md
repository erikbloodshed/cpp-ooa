PR-8. Show the value of `sum` after the following algorithm is executed.
```
sum = 0
sum = sum + 10
sum = sum * 10
sum = sum - 10
```

*Solution:*  
```
sum = 0
sum = sum + 10  // sum = 0 + 10 = 10
sum = sum * 10  // sum = 10 * 10 = 100
sum = sum - 10  // sum = 100 - 10 = 90
```
The final value of `sum` is `90`.
